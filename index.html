Knowledge Architecture

The repository is split into clear sections so each topic can be studied independently while still feeling part of one academic system.

Section A

Concept cards for preprocessing, vectorization, embeddings, and language models.

Section B

Side-by-side comparisons highlighting working mechanisms and trade-offs.

Section C

Workflow diagrams for preprocessing, feature engineering, and language modeling.

Section D

Real-world applications mapped back to the NLP concepts they use.

Section E

Research and industry insights covering modern tools, papers, and frameworks.

Section F

Sustainability and societal impact, including SDG alignment and accessibility.

Section G

Contribution matrix documenting the division of work across the team.

Section H

Reflection notes summarizing learning outcomes and individual takeaways.
